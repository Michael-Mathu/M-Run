# Why Run?

Running is the simplest form of exercise. All you need is a pair of shoes and the willingness to move.

## Benefits of Running

- **Physical Health**: Improves cardiovascular fitness, strengthens bones, and helps maintain a healthy weight
- **Mental Clarity**: Running releases endorphins, reducing stress and improving mood
- **Accessibility**: No gym membership required - run anywhere, anytime
- **Cost Effective**: Minimal equipment needed to get started

## Getting Started

The key to beginning is consistency, not intensity. Start with what feels comfortable and gradually build up your distance and pace.

> Remember: Every champion was once a beginner who refused to give up.
